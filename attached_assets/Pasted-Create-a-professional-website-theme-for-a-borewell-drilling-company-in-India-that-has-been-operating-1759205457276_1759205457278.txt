Create a professional website theme for a borewell drilling company in India that has been operating for over 30 years.

The website must:

Look & Feel

Classic, heritage-style theme that reflects a longstanding, trustworthy company.

Use earthy tones (brown, beige, deep blue, green) with accents of gold for a traditional & reliable vibe.

Fonts should be bold, clean, and slightly old-fashioned (serif fonts for headings, simple sans-serif for body).

The design should convey trust, experience, and authenticity.

Homepage

Large hero section with company name, tagline like “30+ Years of Trusted Borewell Solutions Across India”.

A photo of the owner with machines in the background to immediately build trust.

Short intro about the company’s history and expertise.

About Us Page

Detailed story of the company’s 30-year journey, covering borewell projects across India.

Highlight the owner’s name, photo, and personal message, showing credibility and reliability.

Services Page

List all types of borewell services offered (borewell drilling, compressor works, mud pump machines, tractor-mounted drilling, solar borewell setup, construction base drilling).

Each service should have an icon or photo, with a short, professional description.

Machines & Equipment Page

Showcase various borewell machines with photos (old and new).

Include compressors, mud pumps, tractor-mounted rigs, and other specialized machines.

Gallery Page

Display old photographs of the owner with machines and workers.

Should feel nostalgic and authentic.

Optional: add a timeline format (1990s, 2000s, 2010s, Present).

Contact Page

Simple contact form.

Show phone number, email, office address.

Include a map of India with “We work across all parts of India” highlighted.

Trust Elements

Owner’s photo and handwritten-style signature on homepage and about page.

Mention of 30+ years experience, thousands of satisfied clients.

Use badges like “Since 1995”, “Trusted Nationwide”.

Technical Notes

Responsive for mobile and desktop.

Fast loading, simple navigation bar (Home | About | Services | Machines | Gallery | Contact).

SEO-friendly (keywords: borewell drilling India, compressor drilling, mud pump borewell services).
